# Medicine Facts Knowledge Base

Use these facts as guidance for store-level handling, storage checks, and transfer risk communication.

## Albuterol_100mcg
- Storage: 15C to 30C, dry location, avoid direct sunlight.
- Caution: Inhaler canisters should not be exposed to high heat during transfer.
- Transfer note: Prefer insulated transport if ambient temperature is high.

## Amlodipine_5mg
- Storage: 20C to 25C, controlled room temperature.
- Caution: Keep in original blister/package to protect stability.
- Transfer note: Standard transfer is usually feasible with basic packaging controls.

## Aspirin_75mg
- Storage: 20C to 25C, moisture-protected container.
- Caution: Humidity can degrade tablet quality.
- Transfer note: Do not transfer if packaging is damaged or moisture-exposed.

## Atorvastatin_20mg
- Storage: 20C to 25C, protect from excessive heat.
- Caution: Avoid prolonged exposure above room temperature.
- Transfer note: Feasible in standard pharmacy transport with sealed packs.

## Azithromycin_250mg
- Storage: 20C to 25C, dry place.
- Caution: Verify remaining shelf life before transfer.
- Transfer note: Favor transfer only when expiry buffer is comfortable.

## Amoxicillin_500mg
- Storage: 20C to 25C, dry conditions.
- Caution: Rotation is important to avoid expiry-based wastage.
- Transfer note: Use FIFO principles during inter-store transfer.

## Insulin_100units
- Storage: 2C to 8C before dispensing, avoid freezing.
- Caution: Cold-chain integrity is mandatory.
- Transfer note: Transfer only with validated cold-chain support.

## Metformin_500mg
- Storage: 20C to 25C.
- Caution: Keep away from moisture and high heat.
- Transfer note: Standard transfer feasible under routine controls.

## Levothyroxine_50mcg
- Storage: 20C to 25C, protect from light and moisture.
- Caution: Potency may be sensitive to poor storage conditions.
- Transfer note: Prefer tight packaging control and minimal transfer delay.

## Losartan_50mg

- Transfer note: Standard transfer feasible.

## Montelukast_10mg

## Nitroglycerin_0.4mg

## Omeprazole_20mg

## Pantoprazole_40mg
